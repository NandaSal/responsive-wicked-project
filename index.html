About Madame Morrible

Madame Morrible is the beloved Dean of Sorcery Studies at Shiz University, as well as its regal headmistress. With a specialty in weather sorcery, our dear Madame is revered by the student body as well as by her peers, who admire her power, wisdom, and intellect.

Some of the subjects that will be explored in this year's Sorcery Seminar include but are not limited to:

  • Advanced Abjuration
  • Advanced Divination
  • Advanced Enchantment
  • Advanced Evocation
  • Advanced Illusion
  • Advanced Levitation
  • Advanced Transmutation
